Development of KPI Reports for the team.

My team has been struggling with the record keeping of the monthly action items, so I took the advantage of python and draft something that helps the team not to worry about manual records. The program list all the action items that the team have worked on for the previous month and records the dates based on the changes made in the action item, the team used to record this manually at the end of each month for reporting purposes.
